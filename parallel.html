About “Sync”

The Sync pill shows how the highlight is kept in step with the audio:

  • speech-boundary — your browser’s voice reports character positions. We map those to words, so the highlight follows the audio precisely.
  • timing — if the voice doesn’t report boundaries, we use your Highlight WPM to pace the highlight. If it drifts, nudge WPM or click the current word to re-sync.
